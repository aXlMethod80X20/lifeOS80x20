# Convex Operator
### lifeOS80x20 — Life Operating System for Hybrid Builders

> *Train your mind. Discipline your body. Optimize your risk.*

A single HTML file. No login. No SaaS. No subscription. Open it in your browser and run your life.

Built for the person who works a demanding day job, trains seriously, builds AI on the side — and refuses to let any of it collapse.

---

## The Philosophy

The world splits into two speeds:

**BITS** move fast — software, AI, markets, information. Your mental OS must keep pace.  
**ATOMS** move slow — your body, habits, physical reality. They reward consistency, not intensity.

Most productivity systems optimize for one. This covers both.

The 80/20 barbell: be hyper-conservative with most of your capital and attention, hyper-aggressive with a small slice. Nothing in the middle. The middle is where fragility hides.

---

## What's Inside

**MODULE 01 — BITS: The Latticework**  
Mental models inspired by Charlie Munger and Howard Marks.  
Inversion. Second-Order Thinking. Circle of Competence. Margin of Safety. Asymmetric Risk/Reward. Deep Focus Protocol.  
The goal: a latticework of models you can actually use to cut through noise.

**MODULE 02 — ATOMS: Hybrid Training**  
The physical world doesn't move at software speed.  
Weekly protocol: Strength (Mon/Wed) · Recovery (Tue/Thu/Fri) · Jiu-Jitsu (Sat) · Cardio (Sun)  
Log it. Stay consistent. The body is a long-term compounding machine.

**MODULE 03 — CAPITAL: Barbell Portfolio**  
80% safe/linear — index funds, bonds, DCA always.  
20% convex — asymmetric bets, early-stage, your own projects, skill acquisition.  
Markowitz efficient frontier explained. Taleb's antifragile portfolio mapped.
